Frequently Asked Questions

How do AI Tokens work?

AI Tokens are our pay-as-you-go currency. You buy a pack of tokens and then use them for different features. For example, one AI Interview might cost 10 tokens, and one Resume Analysis might cost 5 tokens. They never expire.

How accurate is the interview feedback?

Our AI is trained on millions of data points from real interviews and recruiter feedback. It analyzes your word choice, clarity, speaking pace, and even filler words to give you comprehensive, actionable feedback that closely mirrors what a human recruiter would look for.

What is an ATS score?

An Applicant Tracking System (ATS) is software used by over 90% of large companies to filter resumes. Our analyzer simulates this by scoring your resume based on keywords, formatting, and relevance to a job description, helping you ensure your resume gets seen by a human.
